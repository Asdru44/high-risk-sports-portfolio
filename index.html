About This Portfolio

This digital portfolio was created for ENC1102 as a way to showcase my research project on high-risk sports. Throughout the semester, I explored the psychological, cultural, and emotional reasons why people engage in activities like base jumping and downhill biking, and what these trends reveal about society today.

About the Author

My name is Asdruval Calle, and I am a student at FIU. I developed this project over the course of the semester through annotated research, presentations, and writing assignments. This website is the final presentation of that work.

Project 1: Topic Proposal

Title: Risk, Reward, and Rebellion: Understanding the Rise of High-Risk Sports

Purpose: To analyze the reasons behind the growing popularity of extreme sports in modern society and explore the psychological and social motivations behind them.

Key Questions: Why are people drawn to dangerous sports? How does social media impact this trend? What are the mental health implications?

Project 2: Annotated Bibliography
